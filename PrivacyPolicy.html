PhotoDupeKiller Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Overview

PhotoDupeKiller is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your photos never leave your device.

Data Collection

  • Photo Access: PhotoDupeKiller accesses your photo library solely to scan for duplicate images. This processing happens entirely on your device.
  • No Cloud Upload: Your photos are processed locally and are never uploaded to any server or cloud service.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage data, or analytics.

Photo Processing

  • All duplicate detection algorithms run locally on your device using Apple's Photos framework.
  • Photo hashes (for similarity comparison) are computed in memory and are not stored after scanning.
  • Deleted photos are moved to iOS "Recently Deleted" album, where they remain for 30 days before permanent deletion.

Third-Party Services

PhotoDupeKiller does not integrate with any third-party services, advertisers, or analytics platforms.

Permissions

  • Photo Library: Required to access and scan your photos for duplicates.
  • Photo Deletion: Required to move duplicate photos to "Recently Deleted".

Children's Privacy

PhotoDupeKiller is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If any changes are made to this privacy policy, they will be updated in the app's next version.

Contact Us

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the App Store.

Privacy by Design: PhotoDupeKiller is built with the principle that your photos belong to you, and only you.
